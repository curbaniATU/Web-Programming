Lieutenant (formerly Captain) Titus

Lieutenant Titus is the protagonist to the two video games Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine and Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine II. Over the span of two games Titus has gone up against many foes, with the enemy factions that appear being Orks (Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine), Tyranids (Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine II), and Chaos Space Marines (Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine, Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine II).
